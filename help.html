How to use it?

Step 1:   Click on the "Get Started" button.
Step 2:   Fill the resume form.
Step 3:   Click on "CREATE" button.
Step 4:   Your resume is ready.

How to print or save the resume?

Once you have created the resume. Your resume will be on your screen in your web browser.
Now for printing the resume follow the following steps.
Step 1:  Click on the three dot option (Generaly available at the top-right-corner). A drop down menu appears.
Step 2:  Find for print option. Click on print option.
Step 3:  A preview dialog box will appear. Find for print option. Click on it.
A PDF file of Resume will be downloaded in you system.

Is it free to use?

Yes, It is completly free to use. You can share your Resume PDF at no cost
because our first goal is to make your work easier.

In which format the resume printed?

The final Resume the you print or save is always saved in .pdf format.
